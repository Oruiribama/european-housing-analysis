# Key Insights: European Housing Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

Analysis of 417 quarterly observations across 28 European countries (2022-2025) reveals distinct market patterns, with Eastern European markets significantly outperforming Western Europe. The data shows market stabilization after 2023's interest rate shock, with recovery momentum building through 2024-2025.

### 2. The 2023 Crisis and Recovery

**Timeline:**

**2022**: Post-pandemic boom (13.08% average growth)
- Hot markets across Europe
- Low interest rates driving demand
- Unsustainable growth rates

**2023**: Correction year (5.95% average growth)
- ECB aggressive rate hikes to combat inflation
- Luxembourg crashed -14.50%
- Germany declined -10.20%
- Western Europe hit hardest

**2024-2025**: Recovery phase (6.63-6.89% growth)
- Markets adapted to higher rates
- Stabilization at sustainable levels
- Eastern Europe maintained momentum
- Western Europe slowly recovering

**Lesson**: Markets need ~2 years to adjust to major rate changes
